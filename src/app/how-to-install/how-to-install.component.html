Directions

  1. Refer to the Application Calculator to determine recommended fill amount for your tire/wheel.
  2. Assure equipment is safely supported such that the weight of the equipment will not rest on the deflated tire.
  3. Position valve stem between 10 and 2 o'clock, attach tube to bottle tip and remove valve core to deflated tire.
  4. Install sealant and reinstall the valve core, DO NOT install more than the recommended amount for high speed applications.
  5. Inflate tire to tire manufactures recommended pressure and reinstall valve stem cap.
  6. Immediately rotate or move/drive equipment to seal any existing punctures, especially if any objects are removed after installing sealant.
